Saving

Why Does Saving Matter?

Saving matters because it will help you significantly when you are at a tough time of your life and you suddenly need some extra money to help you get through that period of time.

By putting some of your income or your weekly pocket money aside into savings can help you feel more confident and prepared to overcome any financial problems or emergency situations.


Set Up Your Own Savings Goal Chart

  1. Write a list of all the different financial goals that you want to achieve
  2. Decide whether the goals are short-term, midterm or long-term goals
  3. Determine how much money you will need to reach your goals and how long you have to reach your money goal. Then, divide the amount of money by month or by year. This will help you figure out how much money you will need to save each month or each year in order for you to reach your savings goal by a certain time.
  4. Think of different ways that will make it easier for you to reach your savings goal. This could be things like reducing your expenses (how much money you spend), earning some extra pocket money by getting a part-time job or by doing chores around the house, or even selling your unused or unwanted items, that are still in good condition, online (like on TradeMe).

By doing all this, you will have a clear target as to what you are saving towards, which will be very motivating. You will also feel very satisfied and proud when you reach a goal.
